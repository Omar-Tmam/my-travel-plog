My Trip to Egypt

Posted on 18 August

Last week, I had the amazing opportunity to visit Egypt. Here are some highlights:

Pyramids of Giza

"Great Pyramid of Giza"

"The Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, built over 4,500 years ago as a tomb for the Pharaoh Khufu. Standing at 146 meters, it was the tallest man-made structure in the world for over 3,800 years. The pyramid is a testament to ancient Egyptian engineering and remains a symbol of Egypt's rich history and culture."

The Egyptian Museum

The Egyptian Museum, located in Cairo, Egypt, houses the world's most extensive collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts. Established in 1902, it contains over 120,000 items, including the treasures of Tutankhamun and numerous mummies. The museum is a vital resource for understanding the history and culture of ancient Egypt.
